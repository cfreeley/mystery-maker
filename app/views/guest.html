Thibault

A high-profile member of the goth space pirates.
  • Public
  • Private

As a goth space pirate, he loves all things dark and morbid. Though there are many who claim this, he is one of the few who can actually trace his ancestry back to Robert Smith.

He manages all intel trades for his gang of space pirates, and has done so for the past fifteen years.

Back in the day, he did an interview for your paper, in which he admitted that he is deathly afraid of bees. Nowadays, he tries to hide that fact, and never mentions the interview at all.
